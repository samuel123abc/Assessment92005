Welcome

New Zealand is home to many unique native species found nowhere else in the world, from the iconic kiwi to the ancient tuatara. However, many of these species are in danger due to habitat loss, introduced predators, and climate change.

Why Are Species Declining?

Human activity has had a major impact on native ecosystems. Since human settlement, over 50 species of birds have gone extinct. Introduced species such as rats, stoats, and possums are among the biggest threats. Conservation efforts are underway, but time is running out.
