Why Are Our Native Species in Decline?

New Zealand is home to unique native species found nowhere else in the world. However, many of these species are under threat due to habitat destruction, introduced predators, climate change, and human activity.

This website aims to raise awareness of the crisis facing New Zealand’s wildlife, showcase the species most at risk, and empower everyone to take action.
